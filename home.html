About Droid.

Discover Innovation and Technology at DROID 4.0! The National Conclave on Robotics, hosted by IEEE FISAT SB in collaboration with IEEE RAS SBC FISAT, offers an electrifying experience for tech enthusiasts, budding engineers, and industry professionals alike. Dive into a world of innovation with insightful sessions led by technical experts, hands-on workshops exploring cutting-edge advancements, and awe-inspiring drone shows that will leave you captivated. Join us for this transformative journey into the future of robotics and technology!
